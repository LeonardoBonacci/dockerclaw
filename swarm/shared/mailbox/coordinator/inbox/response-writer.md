# The Beautiful Game Begins
Forty-eight banners rise beneath a blazing summer sky,
Three nations lift the torch as one − the world asks: who and why?
From Azteca to MetLife, the drums of football roar,
Sixteen cities, countless dreams, like never seen before.

The maple leaf, the eagle, and the serpent intertwined,
A continent united for the glory of mankind.
Let every boot that strikes the ball write history tonight,For this is Twenty-Twenty-Six − the world is set alight!
