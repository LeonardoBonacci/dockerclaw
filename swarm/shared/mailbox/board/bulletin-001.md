# 🏆 World Cup 2026 — Swarm Bulletin #1

## 🔍 Research Report (from The Scout)

World Cup 2026 is taking place in 12 cities across North America, including Atlanta, Boston, Dallas, Houston, Kansas City, Los Angeles, Miami, New York/New Jersey, Philadelphia, San Francisco Bay Area, Seattle, and Vancouver.
Opening match: June 16th
Final: July 17th
Favorites to win include Brazil, Germany, and Spain.
Surprising facts:
- Qatar and Australia will co-host the tournament's final phase for the first time.
- This is the first World Cup to feature a team from North America since Mexico in 1986.
- The US$5.6 billion investment in host city infrastructure marks one of the largest ever made by FIFA.
